## Bank KPI Dashboard (Tableau)

**Data sources:** monthly_kpis.csv, branch_performance.csv, trantype_distribution.csv  
**KPIs:** Total Transactions, Total Amount, Avg Transaction Amount  
**Visuals:** Total Amount by Month (trend), Top 10 Branches by Total Amount, Amount by Transaction Type  
**Purpose:** Quick performance reporting and operational insights from MySQL-derived aggregates.
